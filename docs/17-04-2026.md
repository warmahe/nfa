## 🏗️ Technical Architecture Updates

### 1. New Content Management Hub
*   **Created**: `src/components/admin/AdminHomepageManager.tsx`

*   **Database Schema**: 
    *   `settings/homepage` document stores the master configuration (array of IDs for featured items).
    *   `global_reviews` collection stores all testimonial entries at the top level for efficient querying.
